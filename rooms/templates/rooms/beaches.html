Jericoacoara Travel Guide

Best Beaches

Jericoacoara Beach

Known for its stunning sunset views and vibrant nightlife.

Prea Beach

A hotspot for kite surfing and windsurfing enthusiasts.

Malhada Beach

A more secluded beach, ideal for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

Guriú Beach

Famous for its mangroves and boat tours through the river.

Things to Do

  • Explore Duna do Pôr do Sol: Climb the dune to watch a breathtaking sunset.
  • Visit Pedra Furada: A natural rock formation that's a must-see landmark.
  • Take a Buggy Tour: Experience an exciting ride through the dunes and lagoons.
  • Wind and Kite Surfing: Take advantage of the strong winds and enjoy water sports.

Recommended Restaurants

  • Tamarindo: Offers a great variety of seafood and Brazilian dishes.
  • Na Casa Dela: A charming place with a cozy atmosphere and delicious homemade food.
  • Bistrogonoff: Known for its excellent service and a mix of Brazilian and international cuisine.
  • Naturalmente: Perfect for vegetarians and those looking for healthy meal options.
